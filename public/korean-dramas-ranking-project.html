The Best Korean Dramas According to Diane

My top 3 Korean Dramas of all-time.

Crash Landing on You

I watched it around 2020, pandemic time, and I would say that it's still one of the best Korean dramas out there. The two co-stars married years later.

Itaewon Class

Wholesome plot. Great acting. I love PSJ.

Business Proposal

The plot is refreshing, with funny storylines and beautiful visual actors and actresses. I've felt a lot of "kilig" from the supporting actors as well.
